SOME MORE S’MORES

by Katie Parker
published on September 10, 2013

As fun as roasting marshmallows over an open fire is, I prefer to make s’mores pan style. There are super fast and so delicious!

Pan Style S’mores

  • 10-12 graham crackers (crushed)
  • 1 bag of mini marshmallows
  • 3-4 TBS of butter
  • 3 chocolate bars

You're basically making rice krispie treats, but I'll break in down for you. Melt the butter over medium heat. Add in the marshmallows and stir until they have melted. Add in your crushed graham crackers and stir, stir, stir. Press the mixture into a 8x10 pan and top with some ooey gooey chocolate.
